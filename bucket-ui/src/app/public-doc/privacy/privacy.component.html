Privacy Policy

You can find detailed information about how Bucket works on the Background Information page. It includes how we, the Data-Centric Design Lab, collect, store, use, transfer, and share personal data from you and how you can control it. On this page we present you with the key take aways.

By signing up to Bucket via the Data-Centric Design Lab, you acknowledge and agree that your personal data can be managed as described in this Privacy Policy. We reserve the right to change this Privacy Policy. If we make any changes we will notify you by email and offer you the possibility to re-evaluate your consent. You can always contact us by sending an email to lab@datacentricdesign.org.

A Person, characterised by a unique email address, a name and a password, can use Bucket to facilitate data manipulation from Things and their Properties.

A Person can Consent, or explicitly agree to share a Property, with a receiving Person, Thing, or Group. In this case, the Property becomes a Shared Property that can be accessed by the receiving Person, Thing or Group. Consent can be revoked at all times.

A Group corresponds to a set of Persons and Things.

Your Rights

  • You have the right to request information about any and all personal information that we hold about you, to access all your personal data and receive a copy of it.
  • You have a right to ask us to correct any inaccuracies reflected on your personal data and to update any out of date information.
  • You have the right to erase your personal data, to withdraw your consent to processing and to revoke access.

You can exercise your rights through Bucket. However, if you experience difficulties in exercising any of these rights, or wish to object to our use of your personal information please contact us via email at: j.bourgeois@tudelft.nl
